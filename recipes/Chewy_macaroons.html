Chewy Macaroons

Description

My favourite biscuits and only 2 ingredients needed!.

Ingredients

  • 250 g desiccated coconut
  • 395 g condensed milk
    •

    Steps

    1. Preheat oven to 160C.
    2. Mix ingredients well together, using enough milk to hold mixture together.
    3. Put heaped teaspoonfuls onto greased biscuit trays.
    4. Cook for about 15 minutes or until tops have a brown tinge but are still soft in the centre.
    5. Cool on trays for a few minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool.
    6. Store in air tight container.
