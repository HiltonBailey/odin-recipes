Oven Chips

Description

Quick to make and delicious oven chips!

Ingredients

  • 6 potatoes
  • 3 tbs olive oil
    •

    Steps

    1. Preheat oven to 220C.
    2. Cut potatoes into slices approximately 1 cm thick.
    3. Soak chips in cold water for 10 minutes, drain well, then pat dry with paper towels.
    4. Spread chips onto a baking tray, sprinkle with oil. Toss to coat.
    5. Bake for 45-55 minutes until golden and crisp, turning occasionally.
